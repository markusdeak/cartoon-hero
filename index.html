Grogu

About Grogu

Grogu - also known as "The Child" - is a force-sensitive Mandalorian belonging to the same mysterious species as the legendary Grand Master Yoda.

Grogu wants to play with everything

Grogu's Characteristics

  1. He has a connection to the Force
  2. He loves to play with the control knob from the Razor Crest
  3. He often wants to eat living creatures like frogs, worms or eggs
